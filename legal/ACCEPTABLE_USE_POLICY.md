# ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY (AUP)
**Effective Date:** December 7, 2025
**Version:** 1.0

This AUP defines the boundaries of use for the Aether Platform.

## 1. PERMITTED USE
You may use the Platform only for:
* Legitimate legal research, analysis, and strategy generation.
* Simulation of legal proceedings (Depositions, Hearings) for preparation purposes.
* Management of case files and evidence.
* Professional development and training.

## 2. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
You shall not:
* **Unlawful Use:** Use the Platform to generate contracts or strategies intended to defraud, mislead courts, or violate any law.
* **Fabrication:** Intentionally use the "Adversarial Simulation" features to generate false evidence or fabricated records for submission to a tribunal.
* **Reverse Engineering:** Attempt to decompile, reverse engineer, or discover the source code, "Cognitive Protocols," or system prompts of the Platform.
* **High-Risk Scenarios:** Use the Platform in any context where failure could lead to death, personal injury, or physical damage (e.g., automated sentencing systems).
* **Abuse:** Attempt to circumvent security measures, rate limits, or token budgets.
* **Resale:** Resell, sublicense, or redistribute access to the Platform without authorization.

## 3. PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT
Users must maintain independent professional judgment. The Platform provides *intelligence*, not *decisions*. Relying blindly on AI outputs without verification constitutes:
* A violation of this AUP
* A breach of professional responsibility under applicable Rules of Professional Conduct
* A potential basis for malpractice liability

**VERIFICATION REQUIREMENT:** All AI-generated citations, case law references, and legal strategies must be independently verified before use in legal proceedings.

## 4. DATA PROTECTION
You must:
* Use strong, unique passwords and enable multi-factor authentication
* Not share account credentials
* Report suspected security incidents immediately
* Use privilege scanning tools for sensitive documents
* Comply with all applicable data protection regulations

## 5. ENFORCEMENT
Aether reserves the right to:
* Monitor usage for security and compliance purposes
* Suspend or terminate access for any User found violating this AUP
* Report illegal activities to relevant authorities and bar associations as required by law
* Pursue legal remedies for damages caused by AUP violations

## 6. REPORTING VIOLATIONS
To report violations of this AUP:
* **Email:** abuse@eolagateway.net
* Include account details and description of violation

## 7. CONTACT
For questions about this AUP: legal@eolagateway.net
