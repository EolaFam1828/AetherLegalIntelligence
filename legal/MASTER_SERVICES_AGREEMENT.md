# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT (MSA)
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** December 7, 2025

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by Aether Systems ("Provider") and the Customer identified in the Order Form ("Customer").

## 1. SERVICES
Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the Aether Litigation Intelligence Platform ("Platform") solely for Customer's internal legal business operations.

## 2. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
* **Availability:** Provider targets reasonable availability for the Platform. As a self-hosted system, uptime depends on the Customer's infrastructure configuration.
* **Support:** Provider offers best-effort support. Response times are not guaranteed.

## 3. DATA OWNERSHIP & PROTECTION
* **Customer Data:** Customer retains full ownership of all Data uploaded to the Platform.
* **Output Ownership:** Customer owns all Reports, Strategy Documents, and Work Product generated by the Platform from Customer Data.
* **Security:** Provider implements access controls via identity proxy and firm-scoped data isolation. The platform has not undergone formal security certification (SOC 2, ISO 27001, etc.).

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information with the same degree of care it uses to protect its own, but in no event less than reasonable care. Provider acknowledges that Customer Data may contain highly sensitive legal information subject to attorney-client privilege.

## 5. INDEMNIFICATION
Provider shall indemnify Customer against third-party claims alleging that the Platform infringes intellectual property rights. Customer shall indemnify Provider against claims arising from Customer's violation of the Acceptable Use Policy.

## 6. TERM & TERMINATION
* **Term:** As specified in the Order Form (typically annual subscription).
* **Termination for Cause:** Either party may terminate upon 30 days written notice of a material breach if such breach remains uncured.
* **Effect of Termination:** Provider shall enable Customer to retrieve their data for 30 days post-termination before deletion.

## 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the 12 months preceding the claim. Provider is not liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida. Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Orange County, Florida.

## 9. CONTACT
For MSA questions or execution: legal@eolagateway.net
